William W. Lager

I am an award-winning mission-oriented journalist and project manager working in the space where content, technology, design, and audience engagement meet, and building the bridges to link them together. As a bridge builder within our communities, my work sheds light on how everything and everyone is connected, while invoking wonder and discovery of our world and in each other. I serve our communities in an inclusive and empathetic manner where they are and where they will be, intentionally engaging those marginalized or ignored by traditional media.

Over eight years with MPR News, I worked with teams of journalists to collaborate, create and deploy projects that range from the hopeful to the horrific. Each revealed broken trusts, abuse, inequity and hope, while illuminating the shared humanity that we may otherwise overlook.

Portrait credit: Caroline Yang

As a generalist working across the spaces between traditional journalism roles, I fluidly move between content creation, delivery system development, audience engagement, and back, to build bridges in our symbiotic ecosystem that can, and must, exist between audiences and news organizations. I live for finding new ways for us to build these bridges, while maintaining and strengthening the ethics and the best of journalism traditions.
