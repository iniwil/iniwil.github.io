Mizzou demographics, Fall 2020

The Division of Enrollment Management is responsible for coordinating strategic enrollment planning for the University of Missouri and for ensuring that University enrollment services are effective, efficient, and friendly. In addition, the Division is responsible for maintaining the student information system and for generating data and reports for institutional decision-making. Learn more about the student body profile from the fall of 2020.

Number of students

Of the 31,103 total students enrolled in the fall of 2020:

  • 23,396 were Undergraduate students
  • 6,173 were Graduate students
  • 1,534 were Professional students
