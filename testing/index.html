Agenda Watch

Agenda Watch harvests the documents that public agencies must make available, like notices of upcoming discussions (agendas) and records of their meetings (minutes).

The Agenda Watch platform collects those documents on a daily basis from agencies across the country and provides them to journalists.

A partnership project of RJI and Big Local News

Source Diversity Tracking Tool

Chalkbeat and RJI joined forces to help small to medium-size newsrooms that want to track the diversity of their sources. Source tracking quantifies whose voices are informating the reporting process.

This free tool draws from research and lessons learned from journalists and organizationsabout how to better track source diversity.

A partnership project of RJI and Chalkbeat
